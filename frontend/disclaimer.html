> disclaimer

[automated_indexer]

DarkSeek is an automated search engine that crawls and indexes publicly accessible .onion services. It operates without human editorial oversight of individual pages.

[no_content_storage]

Only URLs, page titles, and descriptions up to 160 characters are stored. No images, files, videos, full page text, or cached copies of any content are retained.

[no_content_moderation]

Indexed content is not pre-screened or moderated before appearing in search results. Search results reflect the state of the Tor network at the time of last crawl.

[legal_compliance]

Removal requests are processed within 48 hours of receipt. See Terms of Service for the removal procedure and contact information.
